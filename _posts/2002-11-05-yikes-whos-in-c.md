<p><a href="http://www.timesonline.co.uk/article/0,,3-469972,00.html">Attack Iran the day Iraq war ends, demands Sharon</a></p>

<br />
In an interview with The Times , Mr Sharon insisted that Tehran — one of the “axis of evil” powers identified by President Bush — should be put under pressure “the day after” action against Baghdad ends because of its role as a “centre of world terror”.</blockquote>
