Barry Hoggard lives in New York and writes about art and politics on bloggy.com. He is the editor, along with [James Wagner](http://jameswagner.com/), of the arts calendar [ArtCat](http://www.artcat.com/), in addition to being its software developer.

He also operates a platform for hosting artist and gallery websites called [ArtCat Hosting](http://hosting.artcat.com/).
